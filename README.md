# 📊 Sales & Profit KPI Dashboard (Business Intelligence Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business performance using **SQL, Excel, and Power BI**.
The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights and build an **interactive KPI dashboard** for decision-making.

The analysis covers:

* Sales performance across regions
* Profitability by product category
* Customer contribution
* Seasonal sales trends
* Impact of discounts on profit

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Perform **SQL JOIN operations** on multiple datasets
* Calculate key business KPIs
* Conduct comparative analysis using Excel
* Build an interactive **Power BI dashboard**
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🗂️ Dataset

The project uses two main datasets:

    Orders Table

   Order ID, Order Date, Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount, Sub-Category
   
    Customers Table

   Customer ID, Customer Name, Region, Segment

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL (MySQL)** → Data extraction & KPI queries
* **Microsoft Excel** → Pivot tables & calculations
* **Power BI** → Dashboard creation & visualization

---

The dashboard includes:

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* **West region** generates the highest sales, making it the strongest market
* **Technology category** has the highest profit margin (~17%)
* **Furniture category** has very low profitability (~2–3%)
* **Discounts negatively impact profit**, reducing overall margins
* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
* Sales show strong **seasonality**, peaking in **Q4 (November)**

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

* Focus on expanding high-performing regions (West)
* Improve pricing and cost strategies for low-margin categories (Furniture)
* Optimize discount strategies to protect profit
* Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs
* Prepare inventory and marketing campaigns for peak seasons
